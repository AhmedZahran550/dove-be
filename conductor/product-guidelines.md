# Product Guidelines

## Documentation & Communication Style
*   **Tone:** Professional and Technical.
*   **Style:** Concise and Direct.
*   **Documentation:** Focus on clarity, accuracy, and providing immediate value to developers and stakeholders.

## Visual & Brand Identity (Backend Perspective)
*   **API Design:** Consistent, RESTful, and well-documented using OpenAPI/Swagger.
*   **Naming Conventions:** Clear, descriptive, and consistent across all modules.
*   **Error Handling:** Meaningful error messages and standardized response formats.

## Quality Standards
*   **Code Quality:** Adherence to established linting and formatting rules.
*   **Testing:** High emphasis on automated testing (unit, integration, and E2E) to ensure reliability.
*   **Performance:** Optimized queries and efficient resource management.

## Technical Principles
*   **Modularity:** Designing components that are independent and reusable.
*   **Scalability:** Building with growth in mind, both in terms of data and user load.
*   **Security:** Implementing robust authentication and authorization mechanisms.
