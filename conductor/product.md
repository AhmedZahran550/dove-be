# Initial Concept

This project is an Industrial/Manufacturing Management System focused on operations, equipment, work orders, and scheduling.

# Product Definition

## Target Users
- Factory Managers / Administrators: Responsible for overseeing operations, managing production, and making strategic decisions.
- Machine Operators / Floor Workers: Directly interact with equipment, execute work orders, and log operational data.

## Primary Goals
- **Improve production efficiency and OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness):** Provide tools and data to optimize manufacturing processes.
- **Digitalize and track work orders and equipment maintenance:** Streamline maintenance workflows and ensure proper record-keeping.
- **Manage employee shifts and operational logs:** Facilitate scheduling and maintain a detailed history of operational events.

## Core Features
- **Real-time OEE monitoring and reporting:** Offer insights into equipment performance and production metrics.
- **Work order creation, assignment, and tracking:** Enable effective management of tasks and their progress.
- **Equipment management and maintenance scheduling:** Maintain an inventory of equipment and schedule preventive or corrective maintenance.
- **Shift management and operator assignment:** Organize work shifts and assign operators to specific tasks or equipment.
- **User authentication and authorization:** Secure access to the system based on user roles.
- **Data import/export for schedules and configurations:** Provide functionality to easily manage system data.
